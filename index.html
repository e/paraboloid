Additional information

Besides hacking, I am interested in energy production, electricity, electronics, and thermal engines. I host here a method to build paraboloids with sheets of paper. If you stick an aluminium foil to the paper, you can use this to build a parabolic solar concentrator and get heat, or an antenna and increase your signal. You just need to print a diagram and cut a paper following the lines and use some glue. To draw that diagram, an integral calculus is needed, but this site takes care of that for you.
